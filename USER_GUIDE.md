# ⚖️ LegalEase AI — User Guide

> **Stop signing contracts you don't fully understand.**

LegalEase AI reads your contract and explains it in plain English — flagging risky clauses, summarising what you're agreeing to, and telling you exactly what to ask before you sign. No legal degree required.

---

## What can I use it for?

LegalEase AI works with any contract in PDF format:

- 📋 Employment offers & contracts
- 🤝 NDAs (Non-Disclosure Agreements)
- 🏠 Lease & rental agreements
- 💼 Freelance & service agreements
- 💻 SaaS terms of service
- 🏥 Any other legal document

---

## The three tools

### 🚩 Analyze Contract
Upload your contract and get a full breakdown in seconds:

- **Red Flags** — the risky parts highlighted by severity (high / medium / low), explained in plain English, with a suggested question to ask the other party
- **Summary** — a 3–4 sentence plain-English overview of what you're actually agreeing to
- **Before You Sign checklist** — specific actions to take before putting your signature down

**How to use it:**
1. Click **Browse files** and select your contract PDF
2. Choose the contract type (or leave it on Auto-detect)
3. Click **Analyze Contract**
4. Wait about 15 seconds — results appear in three tabs

---

### 💬 Chat with Contract
Ask questions about your contract in plain English, just like texting a knowledgeable friend.

**Example questions you can ask:**
- *"Can they fire me without notice?"*
- *"What happens if I want to leave early?"*
- *"Am I allowed to work for other companies?"*
- *"What are my IP obligations?"*
- *"Are there any auto-renewal clauses?"*

Every answer shows the exact clause it came from, so you can verify it yourself.

**How to use it:**
1. Upload your contract PDF
2. Click one of the starter questions, or type your own in the chat box
3. Keep asking follow-ups — it remembers the conversation

---

### 🔀 Compare Two Contracts
Got two job offers? Two lease options? Upload both and get a side-by-side breakdown of the key differences — termination terms, compensation, IP ownership, non-competes, liability, and confidentiality — with a plain-English verdict on which is more favourable to you.

**How to use it:**
1. Upload Contract A on the left
2. Upload Contract B on the right
3. Click **Compare Contracts**
4. Expand each category to see the differences and verdict

## Tips for best results

**Use the right contract type.** Selecting "Employment" vs "Lease" helps the AI focus on the clauses that matter most for that type of document.

**Ask specific questions.** "What does section 4.2 say about IP?" gets a better answer than "explain the contract."

**Check the source clauses.** Every chat answer shows the exact clause it pulled from. Always read the original text to confirm.

**Run it on the final version.** Contracts often change during negotiation — analyse the final draft before signing, not the first one you receive.

---

## What LegalEase AI will NOT do

- ❌ Give you formal legal advice
- ❌ Tell you whether to sign or not
- ❌ Guarantee it has caught every risk
- ❌ Replace a qualified lawyer for high-stakes contracts

For anything involving significant money, your job, or long-term commitments, use LegalEase to *prepare* — then take your questions to a real lawyer. You'll save time and money because you'll arrive informed.

---

## Privacy

Your contract is processed in your browser session and is not stored anywhere after you close the tab. Do not upload documents containing highly sensitive personal information (e.g. passports, bank details) beyond what is in the contract itself.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

LegalEase AI is an informational tool only. It is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. The analysis it generates may be incomplete or inaccurate. Always consult a qualified attorney before signing any legally binding document.
